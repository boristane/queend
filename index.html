Wind on earth is the movement of air on a large scale. In metrorology, we often refer to wind according to its strenght and direction.

The question of wind comfort and safety in urban areas is of major importance in the UK, where strong regulations exist. To answer this question, wind engineers make use of CFD or wind tunnel testing to quantify the impact of buildings on pedestrian comfort and safety.

These studies are based on statistical wind data available at the closest weather stations to the urban area of interest.

It is now clear that a deep understanding of wind climate across the UK would provide better tools to wind microclimate consultants and help them better inform their clients.
